Departments

Aerospace Engineering

Type of Degree: MEng(Project-based, Co-op), MSc(Course-based), MASc(Thesis-based)

Number of Credits: 45

Prerequisites: Bachelor's degree in Aerospace, Aeronautical, Computers or related field.

About the Course: Focuses on the design, development, and maintenance of aircraft and spacecraft.

Industrial Engineering

Type of Degree: MEng(Project-based, Summer Internship), MSc(Course-based)

Number of Credits: 40

Prerequisites: Bachelor's degree in Business, Chemical, Industrial, Mechanical or related field.

About the Course: Focuses on optimizing complex processes and systems in manufacturing and services.

Electrical and Computer Engineering

Type of Degree: Meng(Project-based, Co-op, Summer Internship), MSC(Course-based), MASC(Thesis-based)

Number of Credits: 48

Prerequisites: Bachelor's degree in Aerospace, Aeronautical, Computers, Electrical, Electronics, Mechanical or related field.

About the Course: Covers the principles of electrical systems, computer systems, and their applications.

Mechanical Engineering

Type of Degree: MEng(Project-based, Summer Internship, Co-op), MSC(Course-based)

Number of Credits: 42

Prerequisites: Bachelor's degree in Aerospace, Industrial, Mechanical or related field.

About the Course: Focuses on the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems.

Degrees and their Comparison

Degree Core Subjects Career Opportunities Focus
Aerospace Engineering Aerodynamics, Propulsion, Aircraft Systems Aerospace Engineer, Aircraft Designer, Space Scientist Design and development of aircraft and spacecraft.
Industrial Engineering Operations Research, Systems Engineering, Supply Chain Management Industrial Engineer, Process Improvement Specialist, Supply Chain Analyst Optimizing processes and systems in various industries.
Electrical and Computer Engineering Electronics, Signal Processing, Telecommunications Electrical Engineer, Systems Analyst, Network Engineer Design and analysis of electrical systems and computer networks.
Mechanical Engineering Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Robotics Mechanical Engineer, Robotics Engineer, Product Designer Design and development of mechanical systems and machinery.
