Department Direct - FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: How can I apply for admission?

Answer: You can apply for admission through our online portal. Make sure to check the admission requirements and deadlines on our website.

Question 2: What programs are offered?

Answer: We offer a variety of programs across multiple departments. Please visit our Departments page for more details on each program.

Question 3: How can I contact the admissions office?

Answer: You can contact the admissions office via email at admissions@concordia.ca or by phone at (514) 848-2424.

Question 4: What financial aid options are available?

Answer: We offer several financial aid options including scholarships, grants, and loans. Please visit our Financial Aid page for more information.

Question 5: How do I find my class schedule?

Answer: Your class schedule can be found on the student portal. Log in with your student ID and password to access your schedule.
